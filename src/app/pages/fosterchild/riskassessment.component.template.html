Education regarding sex and sexuality is essential to all foster children and needs appropriate to the child/young person’s age and understanding. Foster Carers will ensure that all children in their care are supervised at all times whilst using the internet to ensure they do not access inappropriate material. Additionally, Foster Carers need to be mindful of the guidance provided by the Agency to Foster Carers on the use of computers within foster homes. Foster Carers need to take responsibility for ensuring adequate parental control security is fully activated. 

Children and Young People will from time to time want to stay overnight at the home of a friend or attend a group activity with a club or association that will require a period away from home, ie Cubs, Brownies, etc. Usually the Local Authorities will permit the Foster Carer to make a decision on the appropriateness of these overnight stays, but this position should be clarified with the placing authority when the Child enters the placement.
